A concrete story

by Alex B. Fine



Justin and I liked the building where we worked.

There were some woods you could see from our office out behind the building. Mostly pine trees.

There used to be a lot more trees here but they got rid of them and put these buildings here.

We used to leave our building together and go into the woods.

Justin and I would take the elevator down and just walk out there.

There was a creek in the woods and we'd walk alongside it.

Justin claimed we could find arrowheads out there because this used to be Cherokee land according to him but who knows.

He'd say just relax your eyes and look at the ground.

I never saw any arrowheads.

Sometimes the creek would flood after it rained and we'd have to walk further up the bank which was fine with me.

Our shoes would get really wet sometimes and I'd worry about getting in trouble at work but I tried not to walk from a place of fear.

One day some other people were down at the creek, surveyors maybe.

We hid from them by walking far away from the creek into a part of the woods where we hadn't been before.

We found something weird.

We found these old headstones.

They were small and you couldn't read the writing on them because they were old. We tried.

We looked at all of them and then headed back to the building where we worked.

We got upstairs and Justin said Oh God.

He motioned for me to follow him and I followed him into the bathroom.

He was touching his privates and he said Oh God.

He said they're all over me.

He unbuttoned his pants and showed me that little deer ticks were swarmed all around his privates.

I told him I think those are baby ticks.

He said fuck fuck fuck.

He started to pull them off and put them in the sink.

I watched him pull off more than ten but there were still a lot of ticks all over his privates.

The ones he'd picked off were crawling back out of the sink.

I said this isn't gonna work probably.

Tears were starting to fill his eyes and I turned around and left the bathroom.

I went back to my desk and logged in to my computer and thought about how maybe I should go tell the surveyors about Justin's privates and warn them about the little gravestones.
